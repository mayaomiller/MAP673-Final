About This Map

This map displays historic locations listed on the National Register of Historic Places (NRHP) across the United States.

Click on a location to view the site name, city, and address. The Legend displays the site type: Historic Place, Historic Landmark, and if it is a location that has been removed from the National Register of Historic Loctions. Enjoy Exploring the Map!

Data source: https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/national-register-of-historic-places-9d3f5
